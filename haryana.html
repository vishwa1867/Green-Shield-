Soil Distribution in Haryana

Best Crops for Haryana

Haryana has Alluvial Soil and Loamy Soil, making it ideal for crops like Wheat, Rice, Mustard, and Sugarcane.

Agricultural Challenges

  • Overuse of chemical fertilizers leading to soil degradation
  • Water depletion due to intensive wheat-rice cropping
  • Pollution from stubble burning

Market Trends & Profits

  • Haryana is India's largest wheat producer
  • Mustard and sugarcane farming have increasing demand
  • Adopting crop diversification can improve profitability
