Soil Distribution in Odisha

Best Crops for Odisha

Odisha has Red Soil, Laterite Soil, and Coastal Alluvial Soil, supporting crops like Rice, Pulses, Oilseeds, and Coconut.

Agricultural Challenges

  • Frequent cyclones and floods
  • Soil degradation and salinity issues
  • Water scarcity affecting non-coastal regions

Market Trends & Profits

  • Odisha is the largest producer of cashew and coconut
  • High export demand for turmeric and spices
  • Growing potential for organic rice and oilseeds
