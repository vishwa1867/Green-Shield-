Soil Distribution in Kerala

Best Crops for Kerala

Kerala's soil is rich and diverse: Laterite Soil (ideal for coconut and rubber), Alluvial Soil (best for rice and banana), and Sandy Soil (suitable for spices like black pepper and cardamom).

Agricultural Challenges

  • Heavy monsoons causing floods and soil erosion
  • Limited availability of cultivable land due to urbanization
  • Dependence on cash crops leading to reduced food crop production

Market Trends & Profits

  • High global demand for Kerala spices like cardamom and black pepper
  • Growing organic farming sector with increased exports
  • Rubber plantations as a significant contributor to the economy
