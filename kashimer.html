Kashmir Soil & Crop Guide

A comprehensive resource on soil distribution, best crops, and farming techniques in Kashmir.

Soil Distribution in Kashmir

Best Crops for Kashmir

Kashmir is known for its Alluvial Soil (ideal for rice and wheat), Mountain Soil (best for saffron and apples), and Peaty Soil (suitable for vegetables and floriculture).

Agricultural Challenges

  • Extreme winter temperatures affecting crop cycles
  • Waterlogging issues in certain regions
  • Soil erosion in hilly terrains

Market Trends & Profits

  • Saffron and apples have high export value
  • Organic farming is gaining momentum
  • Floriculture and medicinal herbs are profitable sectors

Expert Recommendations

Experts suggest adopting terrace farming, greenhouse cultivation, and organic fertilizers to maintain soil health and increase productivity.

  • Drip irrigation conserves water and improves yield.
  • Mulching retains soil moisture and enhances fertility.
  • Greenhouse farming enables year-round crop production.
