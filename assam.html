Soil Distribution in Assam

Best Crops for Assam

Assam's soil is fertile and supports diverse agriculture: Alluvial Soil (ideal for rice and jute), Laterite Soil (best for tea plantations), and Sandy Soil (good for vegetables and mustard).

Agricultural Challenges

  • Frequent floods damaging crops
  • Soil erosion due to heavy rainfall
  • Lack of advanced irrigation infrastructure

Market Trends & Profits

  • Assam tea has a high global demand
  • Growing market for organic rice and mustard
  • Silk farming and horticulture provide sustainable income
